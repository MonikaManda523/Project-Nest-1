Instructions:
Step 1:In a bowl,mix chia seeds and almond milk.Stir well to prevent clumping
Step 2:Add honey or maple syrup and mix again.Let it sit for 5-10 minutes and then stir again to break up any lumps
step 3:Refrigirate the mixture for at least 2-3 hours or overnight until it thickens into a pudding-like consistency
step 4:Serve in a bowl or jar,and top with fresh fruits,nuts and seeds...

Healthy pancakes



Ingredients:self-raising flour, whole meal, whole grain flour, eggs, skimmed milk, berries, yogurt...

Instructions:
step 1:Shift the flours into a bowl or wide jug and tip any bits in the sieve back into the bowl
step 2:Add the egg yolks and a splash of milk then stir to a thick paste. Add the remaining milk a little at a time so you don’t make lumps in the batter
step 3:Whisk the egg whites until they stand up in stiff peaks, then fold them carefully into the batter – try not to squash out all the air
step 4:Heat a non-stick pan over a medium heat and pour in enough batter to make a pancake about 10 cm across. Cook for just under a minute until bubbles begin to pop on the surface and the edges are looking a little dry
step 5:Carefully turn the pancake over. If it is a bit wet on top, it may squirt out a little batter as you do so. In that case, leave it on the other side a little longer. Keep warm while you make the remaining pancakes. Serve with your favourite healthy toppings...


Lemon rice



Ingredients:Lemon, kosher salt, canola oil, black mustard seeds, cashews/peanuts, garlic, green chilli, cumin seeds, curry leaves, chana dal, urad dal, turmeric, basmati rice...

Instructions:
step 1:Combine lemon (or lime) juice and salt in a small bowl, stirring until dissolved.
step 2:Heat oil in a wide large pot over medium-high heat until hot but not smoking. Sprinkle in a few mustard seeds to test the oil--it's hot enough when the seeds sizzle and pop.
step 3:Once hot, reduce heat to medium. Quickly add the remaining mustard seeds, nuts, garlic, chile pepper, cumin seeds and curry leaves, chana dal and urad dal (if using), covering the pan as needed to contain any splattering
step 4:Cook, stirring, until the nuts are lightly brown, 2 to 4 minutes.
step 5:Remove from heat and stir in turmeric, rice and the juice mixture...
