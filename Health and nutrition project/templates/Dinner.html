vegetable pulao



Ingredients:Spices, ginger garlic paste, herbs, rice, mixed vegetables ...

Instructions:
step 1:Add 2 tbsp oil/ghee to a cooker or pot,add whole spices like 1 bay leaf,2 inch cinnamon,4 cloves,4 green cardamom,1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon shahi jeera or jeera,1 star anise,1 pinch nutmeg,little dagad phool,1 smallstrand mace
step 2:When the spices begin to sizzle,add one thinly sliced onions and then 1 to 2slit green chilies saute them well
step 3:When the onions turn golden add 1 and 1/2 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
step 4:Saute well until the raw smell of ginger garlic disappears.donot burn
step 5:Add mixed veggies and chopped mint/pudina.I use peas,beans and carrots most of the times.You can also use potatoes and a handful of split cashews or softend soya chunks if you like
step 6:Next saute everything well until the mint smells good.Usually it takes about 2 to 3 minutes
step 7:Pour 2 and 1/2 cups water if making in cooker and then add 1/2 teaspoon salt as well.If cooking in a pot,pour 3 cups water
step 8:Bring the water to a rolling boil,then add soaked&drained rice.Ensure there is no water in the rice,otherwise the veg pulao will turn mushy ...


Chicken and veggies stir fry



Ingredients:chicken stock,soya sauce,1tbsp pure honey,toasted sesame oil,cornstarch,boneless chicken tighs,canola oil,snap peas,red pepper,onion,mushrooms,carrot,baby corn,ginger,garlic,sliced scallions,warm rice...

Instructions:
step 1:Whisk together stock, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and cornstarch in a bowl until cornstarch is dissolved
step 2:Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add chicken and cook, stirring occasionally, until cooked through, 3 to 4 minutes; transfer to a plate using tongs. Reduce heat to medium-high
step 3:Add vegetables and season with salt. Cook, stirring often, until vegetables are crisp-tender, 2 to 3 minutes (you can add a few tablespoons of water if pan starts to get too dark).
step 4:Add ginger and garlic. Cook, stirring, until fragrant, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
step 5:Whisk sauce and add to pan with chicken. Cook, stirring, until sauce is thickened, about 1 minute.
step 6:Serve over rice topped with sesame seeds and scallions...


Roasted sweet potato



Ingredients:Unsalted butter, sweet potatoes, brown sugar, flour, chopped toasted pecans...

Instructions:
step 1:Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Lightly butter a 9- by 13-inch baking dish. Arrange half the sweet potatoes in one layer, overlapping as necessary, in prepared baking dish
step 2:Top with 1/4 cup melted butter and 2 tablespoons sugar. Season with salt. Repeat with a second layer of sweet potatoes, butter, sugar, and salt.
step 3:Combine flour, pecans, 1/2 teaspoon salt, remaining 1/2 cup butter, and remaining 1/2 cup sugar in a bowl.
step 4:Stir with a fork until clumps form. Sprinkle on top of potatoes.
step 5:Bake until potatoes are soft when pierced with a knife, 55 to 65 minutes, covering with foil the last 10 minutes, if necessary, to prevent over-browning. Serve warm...


Green beans with almonds and vermouth



Ingredients:Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, haricots verts, unsalted butter, silvered almonds, dry vermouth...

Instructions:
step 1:Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add beans and cook until crisp-tender, 2 to 4 minutes.
step 2:Drain and rinse under cold water
step 3:Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook, stirring occasionally, until golden brown, 1 to 3 minutes.

step 5:Stir in haricots verts and vermouth. Cook until haricots verts are warm and liquid is slightly reduced, 1 to 2 minutes
step 6:Season with salt and pepper...


Chapathi egg curry



Ingredients:garlic, ginger, sunflower oil, onion, turmeric, coriander, kashmiri chilli powder, kosher salt, water, eggs, paratha breads...

Instructions:
step 1:Combine garlic and ginger in a bowl; press with a spoon to make a paste.
step 2:Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, reduce heat to medium and cook, stirring, until softened, 3 to 4 minutes
step 3:Stir in turmeric; cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is light brown, 5 to 7 minutes more
step 4:Stir in the garlic-ginger paste, coriander, chili powder and salt. Stir in water, partially cover and cook until the water evaporates, about 5 minutes.
step 5:Add eggs and cook, stirring constantly, until almost set, 4 to 6 minutes. Serve rolled in paratha (or tortillas) with cilantro, if desired...


chicken tikka masala



Ingredients:canola oil, boneless chicken, onion, no salt added tomato paste, turmeric, garam masala, cayenne pepper, chicken stock, heavy cream, kosher salt, black pepper, fresh cilantro, brown rice...

Instructions:
step 1:Heat oil in a large, heavy pot over high. Add chicken; cook, turning occasionally, until browned, 5 to 6 minutes
step 2:Add onion, garlic and ginger; cook, stirring constantly, 2 minutes.
step 3:Add tomato paste, turmeric, garam masala and cayenne; cook, stirring constantly, until spices are toasted and fragrant, about 2 minutes.
step 4:Stir in tomato puree, stock, cream, salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer over medium-high.
step 5:Reduce heat to medium and simmer, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thickened slightly, about 10 minutes. Stir in cilantro; serve over rice...
