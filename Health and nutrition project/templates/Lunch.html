Baked macaroni



Ingredients:chicken stock, olive oil, cauliflower florets, macaroni, salt and pepper, chopped garlic, chopped onion, diced zucchini

Instructions:
step 1:Heat the chicken stock and add cauliflower to it. Cook for 15 minutes or until tender
step 2:Cook the macaroni according to the package instruction and reserve 150 ml of pasta cooking water
step 3:In a skillet, heat olive oil, add garlic and onions and sauté for a minute
step 4:Now add the zucchini and cook until it softens. Add the zucchini mixture to the pasta
step 5:In another pan, heat oil and add flour to it. Cook until the flour changes its color. Add the reserved stock and keep whisking
step 6:Now add basil, oregano, mustard and pureed cauliflower. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes
step 7:Season with salt and pepper. When the sauce cools, puree using a blender and pour it over the pasta. Toss to coat...

Chicken fried rice



Ingredients:Green peas, boneless chicken, garlic, sliced onions, cooked rice, grated ginger, eggs, canola oil, dark sesame oil, carrot, soya sause, broccoli florets, black pepper, salt

Instructions:
step 1:Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat
step 2:Add oil and swirl to coat
step 3:Now add carrots and broccoli and stir-fry for 3 to 4 minutes
step 4:Now add peas, chicken, ½ cup onion, garlic, ginger and stir-fry for 3 minutes
step 5:Add rice and cook for 3 minutes or until it is thoroughly heated. Keep stirring occasionally
step 6:Now push the rice mixture to one side of the pan and cook eggs on its other side, without stirring for 10 seconds
step 7:Now begin stirring for 2 minutes or until the eggs are scrambled
step 8:Add soy sauce, pepper, and salt and sprinkle the remaining onions. Serve hot...

Okra fry



Ingredients:sunflower oil, black or brown mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, onion, okra, coriander, paprika, kosher salt, turmeric, red chilli powder, cayenne pepper...

Instructions:
step 1:Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot but not smoking. Sprinkle in a few mustard seeds to test the oil--it's hot enough when the seeds sizzle and pop
step 2:Quickly add the remaining mustard seeds, cumin seeds and curry leaves (if using); cook, stirring, until very fragrant, about 1 minute, covering the pan as needed to contain any splattering.
step 3:Reduce heat to medium, add onion and cook, stirring, until it is starting to brown, 5 to 7 minutes.
step 4:Add okra, increase heat to medium-high and cook, stirring often, until the "goo" or mucilage from the okra starts to release and the onion darkens, 4 to 6 minutes more.
step 5:Add coriander, paprika, salt, turmeric and chili powder to taste (or cayenne); cook, stirring often, until the okra is browned in spots and the vegetables look very dry, 6 to 10 minutes more...
